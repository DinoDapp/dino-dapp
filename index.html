Introducing DinoDapp

DinoDapp is the landing page for an independently developed blockchain game known as CARDINO! Our goal is to bring education and entertainment together for a global audience. An audience becoming increasingly aware and curious about cryptocurrency, blockchains, smart contracts, non-fungible tokens, decentralized applications, and so much more!

Blockchain Game

There are many games out there but blockchain games are different! Some games feature role-playing elements but players lose when developer support stops. Other games are educational but knowledge is the only takeaway after playing. With CARDINO you get the best of both worlds because players own their game assets and achievements can be preserved forever in the history of the blockchain!

CARDINO

DEVOUR BLOCKCHAIN KNOWLEDGE

Attend the in game CARDINO Academy, with courses that teach you about blockchain technology. Courses range from novice to master and include quizzes, hands-on exercises, and more. Successful completion grants a CARDINO egg token reward for the current period.

DUEL RIVAL DINOSAURS

Every CARDINO egg can hatch a unique dinosaur. Will yours be the strongest? Find out by dueling characters owned by other players in the Colosseum arena! Gain experience win or lose but winners earn more rewards.

SPAR AGAINST ROBOTOSAUR

Before you enter the Colosseum you should practice! Robotosaur, an off-chain AI controlled dinosaur, will always be happy to teach you the ropes. Completing all three rounds versus Robotosaur grants a modest amount of experience.

TRAIN YOUR DINOSAURS

Sometimes dinosaurs get bored and lonely when their trainer is not around. Players may send up to 5 dinosaurs to the Gymnasium to train together. The more dinosaurs you have in training the more experience they will collectively gain!

TRADE AT THE BAZAAR

If you are low on eggs, but not on levels, perhaps it is time to trade? Every type of egg and dinosaur can be bundled and traded in Bazaar contracts. Players can exchange any amount or type of CARDINO tokens from any period. Let the bargains begin!

How does it work?

This game showcases blockchain application development. Features of the game mirror elements of the underlying blockchain technology. In addition to the academy courses, actions in game are linked contextually to implementation. By playing CARDINO, you will be learning about Cardano in small bursts, forming associations that make it easier to remember. Use of visual, auditory, and textual explanations engages more senses to reinforce these associations. The repetition of playing over time will help to create long term memories.

Where do I get the game?

CARDINO is still in development! With a lot more hard work it will become available soon. Once we launch the game, the latest release will be available for download from this website. Be sure to follow the verification instructions on the download page. It never hurts to double check everything related to cryptocurrency!

What is a CARDINO egg token?

CARDINO egg tokens will be fungible tokens in 5 denominations, ironically similar to fiat currency counterparts. The primary difference between fungible eggs and fungible dollars is the egg tokens are finite in quantity. Once egg tokens are minted for a game period no more will ever exist. Each egg token can spawn a random non-fungible dinosaur. Higher egg denominations have limited availability and spawn greater quality dinosaurs with better attributes and bonuses!

How do I get NFT dinosaurs?

NFT dinosaurs can only be created by "hatching" a smart contract within the game. This contract will require a CARDINO egg token and the minimum ADA to process the transaction on the blockchain. The contract will generate an NFT dinosaur based on the value of the egg and securely deposit it in the wallet that provided the egg.

When will the current period end or next period begin?

There is no set date for the beginning or ending of any period. These coincide with development of the game and loosely with the Cardano roadmap. The Triassic period will begin with the launch of the game sometime after the Goguen era of Cardano. It will include 5 denominations of fungible egg tokens which can spawn any of the 5 initial types of non-fungible dinosaurs. A Jurassic and Cretaceous period are planned to introduce new dinosaur types, additional features, and more content in the future.

What are the system requirements?

The game is primarily programmed in C++ by a solitary "dinosaur" engineer currently. Thus it will run natively on any internet connected device. Testing was performed on all popular desktop OS as well as Raspberry Pi. In order to be decentralized a Cardano relay node is required. Thus a modern multi-core CPU architecture and at least 8 GB of ram are recommended.

How much does it cost?

The game will always be free to download. Processing transactions on the blockchain requires a minimum amount of cryptocurrency, in this case ADA. These actions will range in cost from 0.15 to 2.44 ADA as of this writing but is subject to change based on new development. Acquiring tokens from the Bazaar and/or other players out of game may additionally require an asking price in ADA. Currently there are only a few actions which will require ADA, listed below.

  • Acquiring a CARDINO egg token
  • Hatching a new dinosaur token
  • Claiming experience to level up your dinosaurs
  • Trading tokens at the Bazaar
