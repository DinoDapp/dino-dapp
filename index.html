Introducing DinoDapp

DinoDapp is the landing page for an independently developed blockchain game known as Cardino! Our goal is to bring education and entertainment together for the global audience that is becoming increasingly aware and even more curious about cryptocurrency, blockchains, smart contracts, non-fungible tokens, decentralized applications, and so much more!

Blockchain Game

There are many games out there but our blockchain game is different! Some games feature role-playing elements but players lose when developer support stops. Other games are educational but knowledge is the only takeaway after playing. With Cardino you get the best of both worlds! Players own their game assets and achievements can be preserved forever in the history of the blockchain. You can trade, sell, or do as you please because your characters and tokens are yours for keeps!

CARDINO

DEVOUR BLOCKCHAIN KNOWLEDGE

Attend the Cardino Academy in game with courses that teach you about blockchain technology. Courses range from novice to master and include quizzes, hands-on exercises, and more. Successful completion grants a Cardino egg token reward for the current period.


DUEL RIVAL DINOSAURS

Every Cardino egg hatches a unique dinosaur with random attributes but are yours the strongest? Find out by dueling characters owned by other players in the Colosseum arena! Gain experience win or lose but winners will earn significantly more rewards.


SPAR AGAINST ROBOTOSAUR

Before you enter the Colosseum and face player owned opponents you should practice! Robotosaur, an off-chain AI controlled dinosaur, will always be happy to teach you the ropes. Completing all three rounds versus Robotosaur grants a modest amount of experience.


TRAIN YOUR DINOSAURS

Sometimes when their trainer is not around Cardinos get bored and lonely. Players may send up to 5 dinosaurs to the Gymnasium to train together. The more dinosaurs you have in training the more experience they will collectively gain!

When will the current period end or next begin?

There is no set date for the begin or end of any period. These coincide with development of the game and loosely with the Cardano roadmap. The Triassic period will begin with the launch of the game sometime after the Goguen era of Cardano. It will include 5 denominations of fungible egg tokens which can spawn any of the 5 initial types of non-fungible dinosaurs. A Jurassic and Cretaceous period are planned to introduce new dinosaur types, additional features, and content.

How does it work?

This game is a showcase of blockchain application development. Features of the game mirror elements of the underlying blockchain technology. In addition to the academy courses the game provides explanations in context by associating actions with related technology. By playing Cardino you will be learning about Cardano in small bursts and form associations that make it easier to remember. Using visual, auditory, and textual explanations in simple terms helps to reinforce these associations. The repetition of playing over time will help to create long term memories.

How much does it cost?

The game is free to download however processing transactions on the blockchain requires a minimum amount of cryptocurrency, in this case ADA. Currently there are only 3 actions which will require ADA. Hatching a new Dino token, claiming experience to level-up your Dino, and of course trading tokens. These actions will range in cost from 0.15 to 1.44 ADA as of this writing but it is subject to change based on new development. Optionally buying tokens from the Bazaar and/or other players out of game may additionally require an asking price in ADA.

Cardino is still in development! Some more hard work and it will be available soon. Once we launch the game the latest release will be available for download from this website. Be sure to follow the verification instructions on the download page. It never hurts to double check everything when it comes to cryptocurrency!

What is a Cardino egg token?

Cardino egg tokens will be fungible tokens in 5 denominations ironically similar to fiat currency counterparts. The primary difference between fungible eggs and fungible dollars is the egg tokens are finite in quantity. Once egg tokens are minted for a game period no more will ever exist. Each egg token can spawn a random non-fungible dinosaur. The higher the egg denomination the higher the chance or producing a rare dinosaur with better attributes and stat bonuses.

How do I get NFT dinosaurs?

NFT dinosaurs can only be created by "hatching" a smart contract within the game. This contract will require a Cardino egg and the minimum ADA to process the transaction on the blockchain. The contract will generate an NFT dinosaur based on the value of the egg and securely deposit it in the wallet that provided the egg.
