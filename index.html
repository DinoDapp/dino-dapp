Introducing DinoDapp

DinoDapp is the landing page for an independently developed blockchain game for Cardano! Our goal is to bring education and entertainment together for a global audience. An audience becoming increasingly aware and curious about cryptocurrency, blockchains, smart contracts, non-fungible tokens, decentralized applications, and so much more!

Blockchain Game

There are many games out there but blockchain games are different because YOU own your characters, inventory, and progress!

Dinosaurs are born again

Learn | Battle | Trade

This time as characters owned by you their trainer. Players will be able to hatch a uniquely generated dinosaur tokens! Dinosaurs come in various types, rarities, and attributes. More ...

CREATE YOUR DINOSAUR

Connect your wallet and Hatch a smart contract to create your very own dinosaur token! Once you have one or more tokens, players may select their champion in the Gym. The gym also shows attributes and current stats for each of your dinosaurs! A default hero is provided for those without a connected wallet or dino token.

DEVOUR BLOCKCHAIN KNOWLEDGE

Attend the in game School, with courses that teach you about blockchain technology. Courses range from novice to master and include quizzes, hands-on exercises, and more. Successful completion of advanced courses grants power up bonuses for your current dinosaur. Don't have a dinosaur? The introductory courses will show you everything you need to know to get one!

DUEL RIVAL DINOSAURS

Every dinosaur token is unique but will yours be the strongest? Fight various off-chain dinosaurs in the Colosseum arena to test your skill. Then discover your true strength by dueling tokens owned by other players! Battling with the default hero is a great way to feed hungry dinosaurs ;-)

TRADE AT THE BAZAAR

Every type of dinosaur can be bundled and traded in Bazaar contracts. Players can exchange any amount or type of tokens from any period. Let the bargains begin!

EXPLORE THE WORLD

This feature was suggested to add more utility to character tokens by a Cardanian! Development has started for side scrolling platformer levels that allow you take your dinosaur tokens on an adventure to discover new rewards, puzzles, and more.

How does it work?

This game showcases blockchain application development. Features of the game mirror elements of the underlying blockchain technology. In addition to the school courses, actions in game are linked contextually to implementation. By playing DinoDapp, you will be learning about Cardano in small bursts and forming associations. Use of visual, auditory, and textual explanations engages more senses to reinforce these associations. The repetition of playing over time will help create long term memories.

Where do I get the game?

DinoDapp is still in development! With a lot more hard work it will become available soon. Once we launch the game, the latest release will be available from this website. Your wallet public API will effectively be your login, no registration required. If you do not have a Cardano wallet the game will show you how to get one!

How do I get dinosaurs?

Dinosaurs can only be created by hatching a smart contract within the game. This contract will require the minimum ADA to process the transaction on the blockchain. The contract will generate a dinosaur token and securely deposit it in the wallet that initiated the transaction.

What can I do with my dinosaurs?
IN GAME
  • Battle AI dinosaurs off-chain
  • Battle player owned dinosaurs on-chain
  • Attend the school to learn and earn power ups and tokens
  • Trade dinosaur tokens with other players
  • Explore the world as a dinosaur solving puzzles, collecting coins, power ups, and more
OUT OF GAME
  • Sell your dinosaurs for ADA
  • Transfer your dinosaurs to another wallet
  • Participate in existing token marketplaces
  • Tell your friends and share on social media
  • Develop your own DinoDapp
What are the system requirements?

An HTML5 compatible browser is all that is needed. A high speed internet connection will improve your experience. The most recent update of you preferred browser is recommended. Cardano wallet and ADA will only be required to get a dinosaur token. Dinosaur tokens will be required for exploration and to access more advanced courses!

How much does it cost?

The game will always be free to play. Processing transactions on the blockchain requires a minimum amount of ADA. Transactions range in cost and are subject to change based on new development. The game will expect your connected wallet to have at least 5 ADA to ensure everything works. Acquiring tokens from other players out of game may additionally require an asking price in ADA. Currently, there are only a few actions which will require ADA, listed below:

  • Creating dinosaur tokens from Hatch
  • Trading tokens at the Bazaar
  • Claiming rewards from World
