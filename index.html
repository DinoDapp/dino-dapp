Introducing DinoDapp

DinoDapp is the landing page for an independently developed blockchain game known as CARDINO! Our goal is to bring education and entertainment together for a global audience. An audience becoming increasingly aware and curious about cryptocurrency, blockchains, smart contracts, non-fungible tokens, decentralized applications, and so much more!

Blockchain Game

There are many games out there but blockchain games are different! Some games feature role-playing elements but players lose when developer support stops. Other games are educational but knowledge is the only takeaway after playing. With CARDINO you get the best of both worlds because players own their game assets and achievements can be preserved forever in the history of the blockchain!

CARDINO

DEVOUR BLOCKCHAIN KNOWLEDGE

Attend the in game CARDINO Academy, with courses that teach you about blockchain technology. Courses range from novice to master and include quizzes, hands-on exercises, and more. Successful completion grants power up bonuses for your currently enrolled dinosaur.

DUEL RIVAL DINOSAURS

Every CARDINO is an unique dinosaur. Will yours be the strongest? Fight various off-chain dinosaurs in the Colosseum arena. Then discover your true strength by dueling characters owned by other players!

SPAR AGAINST ROBOTOSAUR

Before you enter the Colosseum you should practice! Robotosaur, an off-chain AI controlled dinosaur, will always be happy to teach you the ropes. This helpful tutorial will teach you the controls and actions for your current input device.

TRAIN YOUR DINOSAURS

Do you have more than one dinosaur? Players may select their champion in the Gymnassium. This view also shows attributes and current stats for each of your dinosaurs!

TRADE AT THE BAZAAR

Every type of dinosaur can be bundled and traded in Bazaar contracts. Players can exchange any amount or type of CARDINO tokens from any period. Let the bargains begin!

How does it work?

This game showcases blockchain application development. Features of the game mirror elements of the underlying blockchain technology. In addition to the academy courses, actions in game are linked contextually to implementation. By playing CARDINO, you will be learning about Cardano in small bursts and forming associations. Use of visual, auditory, and textual explanations engages more senses to reinforce these associations. The repetition of playing over time will help create long term memories.

Where do I get the game?

CARDINO is still in development! With a lot more hard work it will become available soon. Once we launch the game, the latest release will be available from this website. Your wallet public API will effectively be your login, no registration required. If you do not have an ADA wallet the game will show you how to get one!

How do I get NFT dinosaurs?

NFT dinosaurs can only be created by hatching a smart contract within the game. This contract will require the minimum ADA to process the transaction on the blockchain. The contract will generate an NFT dinosaur and securely deposit it in the wallet that initiated the contract.

When will the current period end or next period begin?

There is no set date for the beginning or ending of any period. These coincide with development of the game and loosely with the Cardano roadmap. The Triassic period will begin with the launch of the game sometime after the Goguen era of Cardano. It will include the ability to hatch 5 initial types of dinosaurs. A Jurassic and Cretaceous period are planned to introduce new dinosaur types, additional features, and more content in the future.

What are the system requirements?

An HTML5 compatible browser is all that is needed. A high speed internet connection will improve your experience. The most recent update of you preferred browser is recommended.

How much does it cost?

The game will always be free to play. Processing transactions on the blockchain requires a minimum amount of cryptocurrency, in this case ADA. The minimum actions will range in cost from 0.15 to 1.5 ADA as of this writing but is subject to change based on new development. Acquiring tokens from the Bazaar and/or other players out of game may additionally require an asking price in ADA. Currently there are only two actions which will require ADA, listed below.

  • Hatching a new dinosaur token
  • Trading tokens at the Bazaar
Who can play?

The game is designed to be fun for all ages. Children under age 18 years old must be granted permission by their parent or legal guardian first! We believe our content is appropriate for everyone but this game has not been evaluated by the ESRB. Regardless of any rating or content a responsible adult must always make the decision of what they deem appropriate.
