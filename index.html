Introducing DinoDapp

DinoDapp is the landing page for an independently developed blockchain game known as Cardino! Our goal is to bring education and entertainment together for the global audience that is becoming increasingly aware and even more curious about cryptocurrency, blockchains, smart contracts, non-fungible tokens (NFT), decentralized applications (DAPP), and much more! Whether you are a crypto newbie asking yourself "What in the world did that last sentence even mean?" or a blockchain veteran thinking we barely scratched the tip of the iceberg there is something here for you to explore with us!

Blockchain Game

There are many games out there but our blockchain game is different! Some games feature role-playing elements or collectible critters but the players lose everything when the developer stops supporting the game. Other games are educational but knowledge is the only takeaway after playing. With Cardino you get the best of both worlds. More importantly players own their game assets and achievements are preserved forever in the history of the blockchain. You can trade, sell, or do as you please with your characters and tokens. No matter what happens what you earn in game is always yours for keeps!

Dinosaurs are born again! This time as NFT characters owned by you their trainer. Players who acquire a Cardino egg fungible token (FT) will be able to hatch a uniquely generated NFT! These NFT dinosaurs come in various types, rarities, and attributes for the current era. Each era will have a limited amount of eggs that spawn types of dinosaurs that will never be seen again. CARDINO

DUEL RIVAL DINOSAURS

Every Dino hatches with random type, base stats, and rarity bonuses but are yours the strongest? Find out by dueling Dino characters owned by other players and test your prowess in battle! Dinos that are not in training may be attacked but not to worry they will gain experience win or lose. Of course winners will earn many more rewards so if at first you don't succeed ... try, try again!


SPAR AGAINST ROBOTOSAUR

Before you enter the colosseum and face player owned Dino opponents you should practice! Robotosaur, an off-chain AI controlled Dino, will always be happy to teach you the ropes. Completing all three rounds versus Robotosaur grants a very modest amount of experience. Dinos above level 10 or those seeking greater rewards should consider other options!


TRAIN YOUR DINOS

Sometimes when their trainer is not around Dinos get bored and lonely. Send them to the gymnasium to train together when you are too busy to play. Remember this is a no fighting zone so Dinos will be on their best behavior. The more Dinos you have in training the more experience they will collectively gain!


DEVOUR BLOCKCHAIN KNOWLEDGE

Attend the Dino Academy in game with courses that teach you about blockchain technology. Courses range from novice to master and include quizzes, hands-on exercises, and more. Successful completion with a passing grade grants experience to your Dino. Straight A students are known to level up faster than any other Dino around!
