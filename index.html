Introducing DinoDapp

DinoDapp is the landing page for an independently developed blockchain game known as Cardino! Our goal is to bring education and entertainment together for the global audience that is becoming increasingly aware and even more curious about cryptocurrency, blockchains, smart contracts, non-fungible tokens (NFT), decentralized applications (DAPP), and much more! Whether you are a crypto newbie asking yourself "What in the world did that last sentence even mean?" or a blockchain veteran thinking we barely scratched the tip of the iceberg there is something here for you to explore with us!

Blockchain Game

There are many games out there but our blockchain game is different! Some games feature role-playing elements or collectible critters but the players lose everything when the developer stops supporting the game. Other games are educational but knowledge is the only takeaway after playing. With Cardino you get the best of both worlds. More importantly players own their game assets and achievements are preserved forever in the history of the blockchain. You can trade, sell, or do as you please with your characters and tokens. No matter what happens what you earn in game is always yours for keeps!

CARDINO

DUEL RIVAL DINOSAURS

Every Cardino egg hatches with random type, base stats, and rarity bonuses but are yours the strongest? Find out by dueling characters owned by other players and test your prowess in battle! Dinosaurs that are not in training may be attacked but not to worry they will gain experience win or lose. Of course winners will earn many more rewards so if at first you don't succeed ... try, try again!


SPAR AGAINST ROBOTOSAUR

Before you enter the colosseum and face player owned opponents you should practice! Robotosaur, an off-chain AI controlled dinosaur, will always be happy to teach you the ropes. Completing all three rounds versus Robotosaur grants a very modest amount of experience. Cardinos above level 10 or those seeking greater rewards should consider other options!


TRAIN YOUR DINOSAURS

Sometimes when their trainer is not around Cardinos get bored and lonely. Trainers may send up to 7 Cardinos to the gymnasium to train together. Remember this is a no fighting zone so dinosaurs will be on their best behavior at the gym. The more dinosaurs you have in training the more experience they will collectively gain!


DEVOUR BLOCKCHAIN KNOWLEDGE

Attend the Cardino Academy in game with courses that teach you about blockchain technology. Courses range from novice to master and include quizzes, hands-on exercises, and more. Successful completion with a passing grade grants a Cardino egg token. Straight A students are known to hatch the best dinosaurs around town!
NOTE
There is a limited amount of eggs per era so there are no rewards once the current era is sold out. Be sure to be one of the first successful students of the next era to earn Cardino egg rewards!
